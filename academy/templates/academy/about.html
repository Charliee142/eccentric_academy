About Eccentric Academy

Training the next generation of ethical hackers through real-world practice.

Who We Are

Eccentric Academy is a hands-on cybersecurity training academy built to turn beginners into confident ethical hackers.

We focus on real-world attacks, real vulnerable applications, and real defensive techniques — not theory-only learning.

  • ✔ Real-world hacking labs
  • ✔ Instructor-led training
  • ✔ Career-focused curriculum
  • ✔ Beginner to Advanced tracks

Our Mission

To make ethical hacking education practical, affordable, and accessible to students across Africa and beyond.

How We Train Ethical Hackers

Hands-On Labs

Practice on intentionally vulnerable systems designed to simulate real attacks.

Real Tools

Learn industry tools like Burp Suite, Nmap, Metasploit, and Kali Linux.

Career Guidance

Build portfolios, solve challenges, and prepare for cybersecurity roles.

Meet Our Founder

Hi, I’m Peter Charles Habakkuk, a passionate ethical hacker and Django developer. I created Eccentric Academy to train students with practical, hands-on cybersecurity skills that get results in the real world.

My journey started 5 years ago when I realized that most cybersecurity training was too theoretical. I wanted a platform where students could practice real attacks safely, build portfolios, and launch careers.
